Funny Name

The old Anglo-Saxon Kingdom of Lindsey lay between the Humber estuary and the Wash.
When the English shires were established, Lindsey became part of Lincolnshire along with Kesteven and Holland.
The new Part of Lindsey was itself divided into three ridings North, West and South.
The company is based in North Lincolnshire - a unitary authority which succeeds parts of the ridings of Lindsey.
Hence the name North Lindsey IT Services Ltd - we also use the acronym NLIT because it's shorter, sounds techier and is easier to remember.
